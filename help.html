Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I register for library membership?

Go to the Register page and fill out the membership form. Once submitted, you’ll receive a confirmation email.

Q2: How can I borrow a book?

After logging in, search for the book in the Catalog and click Borrow or visit the library to issue it.

Q3: What should I do if I forget my account password?

Click on Forgot Password on the login page to reset it.

Q4: Can I renew borrowed books online?

Yes. Log in to your account and go to My Borrowed Books → click Renew.

Q5: What happens if I return a book late?

Late returns may incur a small fine per day. Check the Library Rules for details.

Q6: How can I contact the admin for issues?

Email us at Communitylibrary@gmail.com.

Q7: Are eBooks available?

Yes. The catalog includes both physical and digital books that can be read online or downloaded.

Q8: How can I suggest a new book?

Use the Suggest a Book form under the Catalog page or email the admin.
